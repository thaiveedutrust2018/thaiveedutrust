As we work to create light for others, we naturally light our own way. We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give. No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted. Never get tired of doing little things for others, sometimes those little things occupy the biggest part of their hearts. From what we get, we can give, however make a life! Our Thai Veedu Trust, during the period of Covid 19, donated the box consisting of 5 Kg of Rice to 60 people, Fresh Vegetables were given to 200 people. Grocery things like Rice, Dhal, Cooking oil, Sugar, Tea Powder were given to 100 people.
